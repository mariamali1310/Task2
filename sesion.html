    What Is IEEE

    IEEE, an organization dedicated to advancing innovation and technological excellence for the benefit of humanity, is the world's largest
    technical professional society. It is designed to serve professionals involved in all aspects of the electrical, electronic, and computing fields
    and related areas of science and technology that underlie modern civilization.
